Susan B. Anthony's House

A museum in the suffragist's childhood home that tells her life through the lens of "living in history" — inviting young visitors to see themselves as part of it.

My role
Competitive analysis · Interaction design
Methods
Competitive analysis, storyboard, user flow, wireframe, exhibition design, interaction design
Team
Laura Kerry (PM) · Jina Jung (UI) · Yiyao Nie (Research)

Set in the childhood home of Susan B. Anthony in Battenville, New York, our museum shows the activist's life through the lens of "living in history."

By inserting themselves into the history around Anthony, young visitors can recognize the history that they currently live in — and think about how they have the potential to shape it.
