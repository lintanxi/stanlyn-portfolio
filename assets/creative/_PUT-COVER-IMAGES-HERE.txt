Creative section cover images go in this folder.

Export each cover SQUARE — 1000×1000 px (or 1200×1200), JPG or WebP, ideally under ~300 KB.

Expected filenames (these match the <img> paths used in the Creative pages):

  estranged-stars.jpg   →  Estranged Stars card
  lotus-baby.jpg        →  Lotus Baby card
  sba.jpg               →  Susan B. Anthony's House card
  dialogue.jpg          →  Decent Dialogue card

After adding an image, open creative.html and, for that card, uncomment the
<img ...> line and delete the matching <span class="ph">...</span> placeholder.

You can delete this .txt file once your images are in.
