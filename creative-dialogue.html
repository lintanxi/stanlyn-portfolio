Decent Dialogue

A chatbot that stages conversations between celebrities — living or not — by deconstructing the way they actually speak.

My role
UX research · UX design
Tools
Python · Adobe Illustrator · Sketch
Team
Jingfei Lin (Programmer)

Decent Dialogue is a chatbot simulating conversations among celebrities.

No matter whether the celebrity is still alive, and no matter what language they speak — as long as some original speeches or writings can be found and translated, we can build a chatbot in their style. By selecting two or more figures, a user can stage a "decent dialogue" between them.

It's built in Python, deconstructing and analyzing different styles of speech to create a playful, interactive experience.
