Lotus Baby

An interactive animation game about two generations growing up under China's one-child policy — where the player is asked to build a "perfect child."

Role
Solo project · master's thesis
Tools
Eko Studio · JavaScript · Photoshop · After Effects · Premiere
Themes
Identity · China · one-child policy

Lotus Baby is an interactive animation game, built in Eko Studio with JavaScript.

It explores the friction between two generations — parents born in the 1960s and children born in the 1990s — inside China's specific cultural context.

Players answer ten questions across different scenarios, all in service of one goal the game sets for them: creating a "PERFECT CHILD." The choices, and the discomfort they create, are the point.
